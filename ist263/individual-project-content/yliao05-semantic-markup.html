Meet Woody

All about Sheriff Woody welcomes new friends who want to learn more about Woody, the cowboy protagonist from Toy Story franchise. We summarize the adventures Woody went on, and introduce his toy friends.

BACKGROUND

The born and growth of Woody can be explored through the introduction of character design, and story conception.

MEET THE TOYS

Each friend of Woody is designed with unique personalities. The toys contributed to the journey of Woody, completing their shared stories.

ADVENTURES

The toys have changed their homes throughout the Toy Story series, but they stayed with and supported one another all the time.
